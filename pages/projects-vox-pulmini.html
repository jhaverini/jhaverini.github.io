Using technology and storytelling to save kids' lives

Vox Pulmini

Native Mobile App | Strategy & Wireframing

Machine learning has great potential to improve health through predictive learning. To enable this, two challenges need to be overcome: humans need to regularly take readings to establish trends, and an enormous data set needs to be collected to train AI algorithms. Vox Pulmini solves both. I worked side-by-side with the developer / founder to sketch out flows, identify pain points, and wireframe an app to collect audio samples.
