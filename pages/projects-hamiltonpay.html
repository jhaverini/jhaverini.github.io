Enabling faster, cheaper, safer payment collection for small businesses

HamiltonPay

Responsive Web | Product Design

Blockchain technology is mostly associated with Bitcoin and NFTs. But it also can enable “smart contracts,” which allow transactions to take place in narrow, agreed-upon circumstances, making payments extremely secure and creating whitelisted pipelines for repeat transactions.

As part of the founding team, I worked on user flows and dashboards for several HamiltonPay products to provide instant, flat fee, secure transactions for small businesses, solopreneurs, and medical clinics.
