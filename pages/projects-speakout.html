SpeakOut

Equalizing access to business English in Bahrain

Work completed for SpeakOut in partnership with Lambda.

Clearing the path to learning

English is the language of business, commerce, and higher education in Bahrain. Families are often are locked out of participating because public schools, while good, don't quite offer enough instruction for functional proficiency.

SpeakOut gives access to low-tuition, high-quality courses, taught by well-trained instructors using interactive modalities to propel students to mastery.

Reducing educators' administrative load

As SpeakOut added locations to meet growing demand, the staff had difficulty keeping track of registration, tuition collection, placement testing, and class scheduling. Manual records were adversely impacting teachers' workloads.

A change from manual to digital record-keeping would help staff focus on teaching and improve follow-through for students.

My role

I was primarily responsible for designing the organization's online registration flow. Working with a wider design team, we also updated the landing page, tuition breakdowns, and course schedule functionality; added payment capabilities; and ensured that the design supported a right-to-left format to be viewed in Arabic.
